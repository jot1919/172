Our Proposed Solution

Daily Check-In & Progress Snapshot

By greeting users with a friendly prompt, this home screen encourages consistent journaling and celebrates progress through:

  • Personalized Reminders: A simple nudge—"How are you feeling today?"—alerts users if they haven't logged a new entry yet.
  • Quick-Access Buttons: One-tap options to Start a new log, View/Edit Today's Log, or View Past Logs streamline navigation for younger users.
  • Progress Metrics & Streaks: Using OpenAI’s ChatGPT models—trained on the GoEmotions dataset—our solution analyzes each child’s responses, categorizing their moods in a visual tracker and offering relevant resources for recurring mental health concerns. Real-time stats (e.g., “Mood increased 10% compared to last week”) and day-streak badges reinforce positive habits, motivating children to check in consistently.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Large buttons and clear labels ensure children can easily update their mood and reflect on daily experiences without feeling overwhelmed.

Personalized Log Results & Guided Reflection

After entering today's mood, the app offers immediate, tailored feedback so children can explore their feelings further:

  • Contextual Prompts: If a child appears stressed or sad, a gentle question ("Does this sound accurate?") helps them confirm or adjust their emotional state.
  • Optional Journaling Space: Users can jot down thoughts or experiences, reinforcing self-awareness and helping them process what might be causing their mood.
  • Encouragement & Resources: Suggesting a deep breath or providing helpful resources (e.g., a short calming video or mindful activity) teaches coping strategies in the moment.
  • Yes/No Confirmation: Simple buttons let children correct the mood reading if needed, ensuring the log remains accurate and relevant to how they actually feel.

Log History & Calendar View

A simple calendar interface helps children visualize their journaling habits over time:

  • Monthly Overview: Each day with a saved entry is highlighted, making it easy to see how often they've logged their moods or reflections.
  • Quick Date Selection: Tapping on any date displays past entries and notes, encouraging ongoing self-awareness and retrospection.
  • Consistent Routine Tracking: Seeing empty days reminds users to maintain their streak, reinforcing a regular journaling habit.
  • Easy Navigation: The top dropdown lets children flip through months or even years, giving a sense of progress and growth in their emotional journey.

Self-Care Break & Mindful Timer

A guided activity encourages children to take short mental breaks when they feel stressed or need a moment to reset:

  • Structured Cool-Down: A clear, five-minute timer prompts children to step outside, breathe fresh air, or briefly change environments.
  • Customizable Duration: Buttons let users add more time or start the countdown right away, catering to individual needs.
  • Mindfulness Reminders: Gentle cues (e.g., "Focus on a tree," "Walk around and look around") help them practice being present and calm.
  • Easy Dismissal: A quick "Close" option respects a child's autonomy, ensuring they never feel locked into an activity they're not ready for.
